This page is meant to show what the rankings will be like after a tournament finishes. The rankings on the right reflect what the rankings will look like after the tournament finishes based on the results displayed on the left.
Changing Results
To change the tournament results, click on a loser and they will become the winner of that match. Additionally they will become the winner of any matches the previous winner had won latter on in the tournament.
Hiding and Showing Rounds
To make it easier to manage the results of a tournament you can click on the "Show round" and "Hide round" buttons on the top left. The "Hide round" button will hide the largest round, and the "Show round" button will bring back the previously hidden round.
